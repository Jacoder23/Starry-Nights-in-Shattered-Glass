What is this?

This is a version of Starry Nights in Shattered Glass, a fanfic on Archive of Our Own. However, the fanfic contains many elements that may interfere with screen readers and downloads. Thus, this is a mirror of the fanfic that attempts to strip the elements that could harm accessibility. This is not the originally intended way to experience the fanfic but it is a fanfic intended to be experienced, so if this helps someone experience it then it'll have done its job.

Be warned that, due to this website being used occasionally for chapters not yet released, there may be spoilers for future chapters if you go looking for them here.
